Understanding Fund Categories

Equity Funds

Invest primarily in stocks. Higher risk, potentially higher returns over long term.

Debt Funds

Invest in fixed income securities. Lower risk, stable returns.

Hybrid Funds

Invest in both equity and debt. Balanced risk and return profile.

Housing Funds

Invest in real estate and housing sector companies. Benefit from property market growth.

ELSS Funds

Equity-linked savings schemes with tax benefits under Section 80C.
